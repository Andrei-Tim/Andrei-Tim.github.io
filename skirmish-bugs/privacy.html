Privacy Policy for Skirmish Bugs

Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Skirmish Bugs ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information. Skirmish Bugs does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information we collect

None. The App has no user accounts, requires no sign-in, and makes no network connection to us or to any third-party service.

Information stored on your device

To make the game work, the App saves a small amount of data locally on your device only, using your operating system's standard storage. This never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. It includes:

  • Your game settings (sound, music, volume, vibration, animation speed, difficulty);
  • A display name you optionally enter for yourself in the game;
  • Your saved game progress.

This data is removed if you delete the App.

Permissions

The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, or any other sensitive device feature.

Third-party services

The App does not currently include advertising, analytics, or any third-party SDKs that collect data.

Children's privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country).

Changes to this policy

If the App changes in a way that affects your privacy (for example, if advertising is added in a future update), we will update this policy and revise the date above.
