How do I play?

Skirmish Bugs is a turn-based card duel between two bug colonies. On your turn, play a card from your hand to build up your hive, defend it, or attack your opponent. You can also discard a card to pass. Win by growing your hive to its goal — or by destroying your opponent's.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an account or internet connection?
No. Skirmish Bugs plays entirely offline and requires no sign-in.

Where is my progress saved?
All settings and progress are stored locally on your device only. Deleting the app removes this data.

The game won't start or is behaving oddly.
Try fully closing and reopening the app, and make sure iOS is up to date. If the problem continues, email us with your device model and iOS version and we'll help.

How do I change sound, music, or difficulty?
Open the in-game Settings to adjust sound, music, volume, vibration, animation speed, and AI difficulty.
