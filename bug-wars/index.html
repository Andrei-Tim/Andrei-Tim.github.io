Skirmish Bugs

A turn-based card duel between bug colonies.

Two colonies. One anthill. Only one queen leaves standing. Outwit your rival one clever card at a time — build your hive, breach their walls, and rule the anthill.

What it's about

Skirmish Bugs is a fast, easy-to-learn card duel. Manage your resources, play a card each turn to build, attack, or defend, and race to grow your hive — or smash your opponent's to rubble before they get yours.

Features

  • Quick duels built for mobile — perfect for a coffee break.
  • Smart AI opponents with adjustable difficulty.
  • 30 distinct cards: builders, warriors, mages, and dirty tricks.
  • Clean, colorful art and a one-handed landscape interface.
  • Plays fully offline. No account, no sign-in, no nonsense.
